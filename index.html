Hi, I'm Mark and I Design and Research human experiences

Mark Lester Coscolluela Lacsamana published his first website in 2003. He's currently a Senior Product Designer for Kalibrr where he helps lead the product design team and solve problems on a day to day basis as wel as contribute articles to the Kalibrr Design Blog. Before Kalibrr he was a Product Designer for OLX Philippines, designing their mobile apps and crafting their design systems. He's also an active speaker and coach having spoken at Junior Form Function and Class as well as Form Function and Class. He has been an active part of the Philippine Web Designers Organization and UX Philippines as well as founder of Moving Mobile Forward and Type Philippines. Besides his work in the tech community Mark also helps out in advocacies on Human Rights and Public health.

Mark also dabbles in calligraphy and penmanship, having his work featured in the film 'Ignacio'. If you'd like to collaborate on a project or just want to say Hi feel free to reach out.
